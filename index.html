Our Story

A cup poured with intention, every single time.

Coffee Shop began as a single roaster and a handful of tables in Khadakpada — a place for neighbours to slow down. Today the ritual hasn't changed: green beans are roasted in small batches, milk is steamed to order, and nothing sits waiting under a heat lamp.

We built this room for long mornings and longer conversations — warm wood, low light, and the quiet hiss of the espresso machine doing its work.

  • ✓ Single-origin, small-batch roasted beans
  • ✓ Fresh brewing — nothing pre-made or reheated
  • ✓ A comfortable, unhurried ambience
